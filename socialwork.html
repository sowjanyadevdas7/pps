    Tree Plantation

    Sanchalana Foundation takes a sustainability approach to reduce risk and also extends its wings to protect the flora and fauna by conserving the biodiversity

    "From past 4 years, every year on the occasion of Kargil Vijay Diwas Sanchalana trust has planted 527 plants, In the memory of 527 Kargil Martyrs."

    Supporting Physically Challenged

    "Mega Cause Run 5K was a very special promising event witnessed by us along with our associate partner of the event Paralympic Committee of India. On the Birth anniversary of Shahid Bhagath Singh , The event was held at Kanteerava Stadium Bangalore in support of Para sportspersons. This event was graced by various dignitaries like Union Minister Sri. D.V Sadananda Gowda , Home Minister Sri. Ramalinga Reddy , Sports Minister Sri. Abhayachandra Jain , Paralympian H N Girisha, Former Hockey Captain Sri. Ashish Ballal , International Cricketer Sunil Joshi and thousands of Volunteers actively participated in the event and pledged their support.”

    Drinking Water to Government School

    Pure drinking water is a fundamental human need. Each person of earth requires atleast 4-5 litres of clean, safe drinking water everyday.

    "Our Organization along with Mitsubishi Electric India Pvt. Ltd. donated pure drinking water unit to Government Schools in Bangalore to provide child friendly, safe and sustainable drinking water for good health and good future of kids."

    Free Health Camp Service

    "As the saying goes “ Don’t wish for a perfect life, wish for a healthy life “we under the joint association with Mitsubishi Electric India Pvt. Ltd. has organised health camps at Kabbala and Ramohalli villages of Bangalore Rural with an sole intention of promoting community health and to educate the society. The main aim of Sanchalana is to provide preventive health care. We also provide basic treatment to the children, women etc."

    Free Ambulance Service

    The complexities of real world , economic instability and shortage of health care facilities amidst pandemic outburst were some of the challenges that drove us to successfully launch this initiative.

    "We have recently taken up an initiative to start a free ambulance service for the people who are in need of emergency healthcare services 24X7. This initiative has been taken with a sheer determination to help patients who are seeking for hospitalisation."

    Blood Donation Camp

    A blood donation camp was organized by Sanchalana in association with Jan Sagar Samstha & Indian Red Cross Society at Unnathi Campus Udupi. Within a short duration we were able to collect blood from hundreds of enthusiastic donors and volunteers.
