ABOUT ME

Prem Prasad Shetty B.A.L.,LL.B.,MBA

Born in a traditional household family of agriculturists and teachers and later with a Degree in Law and Management our Founder has been active in building his dream mission of “Nar Seva hi Narayana Seva” (Service to Human is Service to God). He has been working tirelessly to create a Vibrant India which can meet the aspirations of millions of youth and the needy in the country. .........Employment for the youth, Health for all, preserving the Environment, supporting the Physically Challenged, encouraging Rural Sports and various social issues are the primary objective of the organisation that he been driving in the last few years. From a student activist and lawyer to a social activist and educationist, the dream of our founder is to create a happy society wherein equal opportunities are provided to all which lets them grow. Our Founder always believes that “Being one of the world’s youngest Nations, India’s demographic dividend should be harnessed well to create a skilful and strong workforce which can turn the lives of millions of youth who are the real treasure of this society. It is also our bounded duty to support the under privileged, backward and other needy in whatever way we can as everything cannot be done by the elected governments alone. We need the people, the Corporate and the Governments to join hands and uplift the living standards of our people in creating this dream into a reality”.
